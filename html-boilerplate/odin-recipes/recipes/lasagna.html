Lasagna recipe

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingrediants

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two close of garlic are cooked witht he mean to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato Products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatos, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar and subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and Seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley.
  • Lasagna Noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't oozse out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
